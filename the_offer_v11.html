Artist Agreement — Version 1.0 · March 2026

Please read these terms carefully before submitting your work to The Offer. By submitting, you agree to be bound by these terms. This agreement should be reviewed by a solicitor before signing any formal contract.

1. About The Offer

The Offer is a New Zealand-based platform that connects artists who are Christian with churches and faith communities. We act as your agent — we curate, list, and facilitate the sale and licensing of your work to church customers. We are not the buyer; we are the intermediary.

2. Your Rights

You retain full copyright and moral rights over all works you submit. Listing your work with The Offer does not transfer ownership. You grant The Offer a non-exclusive licence to display, market, and facilitate the licensing of your works to church customers under the terms you approve.

3. Licensing to Churches

Works are licensed to churches under one of the following use categories:

  • Digital Display — projection, screen use within church gatherings
  • Print — Personal — single non-commercial print for individual use
  • Print — Congregational — printed materials for church community use
  • Physical Hire — original work hired for display in a church space for an agreed period

No commercial resale rights are granted. Churches may not sublicense, modify, or use your work for commercial purposes without your written consent.

4. Commission and Payment

The Offer retains a commission on each sale. Commission rates are agreed individually and confirmed in writing before your works are listed. You will receive payment via bank transfer within 20 working days of a completed sale. A minimum payment threshold applies — details provided on onboarding.

5. Your Responsibilities

  • You warrant that you own full copyright in all submitted works
  • You warrant that submitting and licensing your works does not infringe any third-party rights
  • You are responsible for providing accurate image files at the resolution specified during onboarding
  • You agree to notify The Offer promptly if any work is sold, licensed, or exhibited elsewhere in a way that may affect its availability

6. Removing Your Work

You may request removal of any work from the catalogue at any time by contacting us in writing. Removal takes effect within 10 working days. Works already sold or licensed prior to removal remain subject to those licence terms.

7. Conduct and Values

The Offer is a values-aligned platform. We reserve the right to decline or remove works that conflict with our curatorial direction or the platform's mission to serve faith communities. We will always communicate clearly if this arises.

8. Disputes

This agreement is governed by the laws of New Zealand. Any disputes will first be referred to mediation before any formal proceedings.

9. Updates to These Terms

We may update these terms from time to time. We will notify listed artists of any material changes with at least 30 days notice.

Note: These terms are a working draft. Before entering into a formal agreement, both parties are encouraged to seek independent legal advice. The Offer will provide a formal Artist Licensing Agreement for signature prior to any works being listed.

Digital Download Licence Terms

Licensing & Permitted Use

Each download comes with artist notes — a short text that opens a door into the work. We encourage you to share these with your congregation rather than letting the image speak alone and unintroduced.

By purchasing a digital download from The Offer, you agree to the following terms.

What you receive

A non-exclusive, non-transferable licence to use the downloaded image file within your church community for the permitted uses described below. You do not acquire ownership or copyright in the work.

Permitted uses

  • Digital Display — projection or screen display during church gatherings, services, and events
  • Print — Congregational — printed in bulletins, newsletters, order of service, or printed materials distributed within your congregation
  • Internal Digital Use — shared via your church's internal digital channels

Not permitted

  • Resale, sublicensing, or redistribution of the image file to any third party
  • Use on public social media, public-facing websites, or external publications without purchasing an extended licence
  • Modification, cropping, or alteration of the artwork without the artist's written consent
  • Commercial use of any kind

Intellectual property

All copyright and moral rights in the artwork remain with the artist at all times. You must credit the artist by name wherever the work is displayed or printed, using the format: "[Title], [Artist Name], courtesy of The Offer."

By completing your purchase you confirm that you have read and agree to these licence terms.
